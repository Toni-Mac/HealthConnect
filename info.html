Outbreak Information
Major Outbreaks
Coronavirus

This is a contagious respritory virus that infects the lungs. Common symptoms include chills, fever of at least 103.0 and trouble breathing. Although there is a 3% death rate, many people have contracted the disease and WHO (the World Health Organization) has labeled this outbreak as a world pandemic.

The CDC suggests that everyone washes their hands often for at least 20 seconds to help prevent infection and spread of the virus. They ask anyone who has come into contact with possible infection to self quarintine and get tested for the sickness. A face mask is only suggested for those who have the illness.

Minor Outbreaks
common cold

The common cold is a respratory virus that has varying symptoms and signs. Symptoms range from soar throat,cough,sinus congestion and/or headach.

The CDC suggests washing hands often and taking cold medicin to help reduce the effects of the illness.

Business Closings
Some business have closed in areas that have been infected by the coronavirus. All business travel is restricted at this time until further notice.

Due to the coronavirus all schools have been closed until further notice.
